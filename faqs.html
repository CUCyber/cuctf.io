  • Where is the event being held?
    CUCTF 2020 is being held online.
  • When is the event?
    CUCTF 2020 is taking place October 3rd, 2020.
  • Who is eligible for the event?
    Only university and college teams are eligible for CUCTF 2020.
  • How many people are allowed on a team?
    Team sizes do not matter for CUCTF 2020, any amount of university students can join a team.
  • Do we get anything for winning?
    Yes! We'll announce the prizes and update this FAQ once we have more information on awards. In 2019 winners received trophies along with cash prizes.
  • What is the CTF bootcamp's purpose?
    The CTF bootcamp serves to allow participating schools to get more students involved at CUCTF 2020. These additional members will solve basic CTF problems guided by CU Cyber mentors and have the opportunity to have a "CTF experience" regardless of their background in cyber security!

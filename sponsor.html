Sponsor

Sponsoring CUCTF is a great way for you to connect with top cybersecurity-focused students studying at various universities across the southeast.

By sponsoring, you can:
  • Connect with top students dedicated to excelling in the field of cybersecurity, and get early access to resumes and portfolios
  • Foster relationships with university cybersecurity clubs across the southeast
  • Opportunity to be involved in extra activites throughout the competition (VR Lab, CTF Bootcamp)
  • For title sponsor, the opportunity to be the keynote speaker

If you are interested in helping sponsor this event, please contact us at sponsor@cuctf.io.

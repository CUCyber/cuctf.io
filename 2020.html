CUCTF 2020 was our second time hosting a capture the flag event, and it was a blast! From the supportive sponsors, numerous excited universities, and excelent Task Force, the atmosphere was electric. We can't wait for CUCTF 2021!

The event included challenges in:

  • Reverse Engineering
  • Binary Exploitation
  • Web Exploitation
  • Cryptography
  • Forensics
  • Kernel

The event also included challenges created by our sponsers:

  • Crowdstrike
  • Runcode
